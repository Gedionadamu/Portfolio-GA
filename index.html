About Me

Hi, my name is Gedion Adamu. I'm a Full Stack Web Developer and Web Designer based out of PA, and I build digital products. As a designer, my goal isn't to just make the web prettier. It's to create an interaction between visitor and brand, to communicate a message, tell a story, or help the visitor better understand the solution, value, and company And as a developer, I want to use the technology that enables me to quickly create a working solution while maintaining the spirit of the product and without sacrificing the user experience. My fascination with web development began in 2013, when, rather than waiting for the developer we had hired, I decided to update my company's website myself, which proved to be more difficult than I had anticipated. That event helped me sharpen my problem solving skills and taught me the value of hacking together a solution until I can produce a better one.
